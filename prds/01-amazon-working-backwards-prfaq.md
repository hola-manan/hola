# 1. Amazon "Working Backwards" PR/FAQ — narrative / customer-first process

You write a fake **press release** announcing the finished product (from the
customer's point of view, on launch day), followed by an **FAQ** answering the
hard questions execs and engineers will ask. You only build once the story is
compelling.

- **Process it embodies:** start from the outcome and customer value, work
  backwards to requirements. Forces clarity on "why this matters" before any spec.
- **Best for:** new/ambitious bets, 0→1 products, getting exec buy-in. Used for
  Kindle, AWS, Alexa; adapted by Stripe, Notion, Shopify.

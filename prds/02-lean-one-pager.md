# 2. The lean one-pager — lightweight / fast-iteration process

A single page in plain English: problem, who it's for, proposed solution,
success metric, and what's out of scope. Written *early* — before design, build,
or funding.

- **Process it embodies:** minimize upfront documentation, decide fast, iterate.
  The PRD is a conversation-starter, not a contract.
- **Best for:** startups, fast-moving teams, small features where heavy specs
  would be overkill.

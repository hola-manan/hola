# 5. The AI / ML product spec — experiment-driven, probabilistic process

A PRD that adds the things classic specs don't cover: data sources and quality,
model behavior and eval criteria, acceptable error/hallucination rates,
human-in-the-loop fallbacks, and guardrails. Requirements are expressed as
**measurable quality thresholds and evals**, not deterministic "given X, do Y."

- **Process it embodies:** you can't fully specify behavior in advance, so you
  define success as evaluation targets and iterate on data/prompts/models.
- **Best for:** anything LLM- or ML-powered, where output is probabilistic.

**Claim:** Once daily protein is adequate, distributing it across several moderate doses
(~3–5 meals of ~0.4 g/kg each) stimulates muscle protein synthesis more evenly than skewing
it into one or two large meals.

**Evidence:** Moderate protein doses every ~3 hours produced a higher, more sustained MPS
response than the same total given as fewer large or many small feedings (Areta 2013); ISSN
endorses even distribution (Jäger 2017).

**Takeaway:** Aim for roughly 3–5 protein-containing meals across the day, each with a solid
protein dose. This is a secondary optimization — total intake comes first — but it's an easy
win. The coach can suggest it to users who hit their protein total in only one or two meals.

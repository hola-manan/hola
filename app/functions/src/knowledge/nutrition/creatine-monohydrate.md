**Claim:** Creatine monohydrate is the most effective, best-evidenced supplement for
increasing high-intensity performance and lean mass over time. It is safe and well-tolerated
at ~3–5 g/day long term.

**Evidence:** The ISSN position stand concludes creatine monohydrate reliably increases
high-intensity exercise capacity and training-related lean mass, and is safe up to ~30 g/day
for years in healthy people (Kreider 2017).

**Takeaway:** A flat ~3–5 g/day (no loading phase needed) is a cheap, high-confidence
recommendation for almost anyone training for strength or size; benefits accrue as muscle
creatine stores saturate over ~2–4 weeks. Most other supplements have far weaker evidence.
The coach can recommend creatine confidently while treating flashier supplements skeptically.

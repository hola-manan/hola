**Claim:** Muscle growth rises with weekly hard-set volume in a graded, dose-response
fashion. Roughly 10–20 hard sets per muscle group per week drives near-maximal hypertrophy
for most trainees.

**Evidence:** A meta-analysis of 15 studies found a significant, graded effect of weekly
sets on muscle size — each additional weekly set was associated with a further increase in
growth, with a threshold around ~10 sets for near-maximal gains (Schoenfeld 2017).

**Takeaway:** Aim for ~10–20 direct working sets per muscle per week. This is exactly the
window the app already encodes in `MUSCLE_RANGES` (per-muscle optimal set ranges) — cards
and code agree. When weekly volume for a muscle sits below its range, adding sets is the
highest-yield change; the coach should surface under-trained muscles by name and nudge
volume up toward the range.

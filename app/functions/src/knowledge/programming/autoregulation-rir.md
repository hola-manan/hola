**Claim:** Fixed loads ignore day-to-day fluctuation in strength and recovery.
Autoregulation — adjusting load or reps to hit a target effort (reps in reserve / RPE) —
matches the stimulus to how the athlete actually shows up that day.

**Evidence:** Because benefit is captured near failure rather than at a fixed %1RM (Refalo
2023), targeting an RIR band is a defensible way to prescribe effort; ACSM endorses adjusting
prescription to individual response (ACSM 2009).

**Takeaway:** Prescribe a target RIR (e.g. 2 RIR) and let the exact weight float with daily
readiness. On a strong day the user earns more reps or load at that effort; on a depleted
day they do less — automatically. This dovetails with the app's readiness signal: a
low-readiness day is exactly when autoregulation should pull load back (~12%, as the app
already does).
